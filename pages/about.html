About ESAFE

ELECTRICAL SAFETY AUTHORITY FIELD EVALUATION

ESAFE offers unparalleled expertise in on-site electrical safety inspections for low volume and specialized electrical equipment. We are proud of our long history in electrical safety, and the experience that we offer our customers. ESAFE has the most highly qualified team in the country. Our inspectors are trained in OESC and have on average, 25 years of experience in the field. Our inspectors are backed by a support team comprised of dedicated and solution-oriented engineers, and a customer focused administration team. ESAFE completes more field evaluations than anyone else in Canada – more than 90,000 field evaluations each year! Our knowledge of Canadian Codes and Standards is unmatched, and our label is respected and accepted in every “Authority Having Jurisdiction” across Canada. ESAFE serves companies in Canada as well as around the world. We are committed to responding to your inspection request within two business days. In emergency situations we can often respond faster – don’t hesitate to let us know your requirements!

Accredited by the Standards Council of Canada

ESAFE is accredited by the Standards Council of Canada (SCC), and we continuously meet its rigorous audits for technical process and proficiency. As an accredited agency, ESAFE is authorized to approve and label electrical equipment for safety. Once your equipment bears our label, it can be legally used or sold anywhere in Canada.

A Proud History of Electrical Safety

ESAFE has its roots in an organization that has been a Canadian leader in electrical safety since 1915. Having earned a reputation of excellence in electrical safety over 100 years, ESAFE has evolved to become an independent field evaluation and certification agency, and operates at arm’s length of the regulator. Each year, ESAFE serves more than 8,000 customer accounts. Our aim is to find innovative solutions to ensure the safe use of approved equipment, helping your company to maintain productivity in an efficient and cost-effective way.
