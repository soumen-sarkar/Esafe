ONLINE APPLICATION FOR ESAFE INSPECTION

Welcome to the online application form. Here you can request an inspection for certification from an ESAFE inspector. Before completing and sending this form, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • All online applications are processed by our Call Center during our office hours.
  • Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 7:30AM – 4:30PM ECT. Any forms received after 2:00PM may not be processed until the following business day.
  • Scheduling is arranged by the inspector. Our inspectors make it their priority to contact you within 2 business days from the day your submitted application form was processed.
  • Signatures on this online form, or received by email are optional. If you are faxing an application, it is required that you provide a signature.
  • If your request is of an urgent nature, please call us at 1-800-559-5356 and speak to a representative. Our office is open Monday to Friday from 7:30am to 4:30pm Eastern Standard Time.
  • This application form does NOT apply for Hazardous Locations. In order to book a Hazardous Locations inspection, please contact us directly.

Not interested in filling out the web version? Our application is also available in print PDF format. You can find the print version here.
