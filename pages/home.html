WELCOME TO ESAFE

ESA Field Evaluation offers unparalleled expertise in on-site electrical safety inspections for low volume and specialized electrical equipment. We are proud of our long history in electrical safety and the experience that we offer our customers. Field evaluation is what we do exclusively, and we make it our business to be the best.

ESAFE completes more field evaluations than anyone else in Canada – more than 90,000 field evaluations each year. ESAFE is accredited by the Standards Council of Canada (SCC), continuously meeting its rigorous audits for technical process and proficiency. As an accredited agency, ESAFE is authorized to approve and label electrical equipment for safety. Once your equipment bears our label, it can be legally used or sold anywhere in Canada. ESAFE inspectors are trained in OESC and have on average, 25 years of experience in the field, and are fully supported by a team of dedicated and solution-oriented engineers.

ESAFE has its roots in an organization that has been a Canadian leader in electrical safety since 1915. Having earned a reputation of excellence in electrical safety for over 100 years, ESAFE has evolved to become an independent Field Evaluation and Certification agency and operates at arm’s length from the regulator.

ABOUT ESAFE

ESA is responsible for administering specific regulations related to the Ontario Electrical Safety Code (OESC), licensing Electrical Contractors and Master Electricians, electricity distribution system safety, and electrical products safety.
